2. **Z-SCOREâ„¢ Algorithm processes the data:**
   - **Phase 1: The Sieve** - Filters 30,000 products down to ~100-500 candidates using hard filters (ingredient match, dietary compliance, safety, availability)
   - **Phase 2: The Scorer** - Ranks candidates using a 100-point scoring system:
     - Ingredient quality & dosage (0-35 points)

   - **Phase 4: Diversity** - Ensures top 5 recommendations cover different ingredient categories (builds stacks, not synonym lists)

3. **User receives top 5 personalized recommendations** with:
   - Z-SCOREâ„¢ rating (0-100)

   - Links to purchase from trusted retailers

4. **User clicks through to retailer** and makes purchase (if desired)
